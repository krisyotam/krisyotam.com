In 1967 susanna was admitted to McLean Hospital after overdosing on aspirin, then chasing it with a bottle of vodka. Once released she 
has a meeting with her psychiatrist Dr. Melvin Potts. Who recommends she spend some time at Claymoore Pschiatric Hospital.
