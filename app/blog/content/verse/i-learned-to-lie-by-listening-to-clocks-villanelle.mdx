# Opening Remarks 
This villanelle is one of my first attempts at writing a ai assisted poem. I do not support the use of AI in poetry for the most part, however for sparse usage such as 
correcting grammar, spelling, and formatting I do not mind. Or in this case a slight extension by having it give me a opening line. 

# Opening Line


<PoemBox>
I learned to lie by listening to clocks
</PoemBox>
