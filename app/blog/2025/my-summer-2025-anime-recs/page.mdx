# Background

For those unaware, this article is on the subject of a battle between Satoru Gojo & Ryomen Sukuna. This battle takes place
in Shinjuku, Tokyo, on December 24th, which is the in-universe anniversary of the death of [Suguru Geto](https://jujutsu-kaisen.fandom.com/wiki/Suguru_Geto).

Gojo was sealed in the Prison Realm on October 31 during the Shibuya Incident and was unsealed approximately 19 days later, around November 19. After being released,
Gojo went to confront Kenjaku. Upon confronting Kenjaku, Gojo fully intended to kill him.
