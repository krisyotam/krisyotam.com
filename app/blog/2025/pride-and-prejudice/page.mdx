# Overview 
A spirited young woman navigates love, class, and reputation in 19th-century England, ultimately learning that pride and 
prejudice—both her own and others'—must be overcome to find true understanding and affection.

## Key Themes

- Marriage and relationships
- Pride and prejudice
- Family dynamics
- Gender roles
