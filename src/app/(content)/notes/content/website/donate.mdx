I'm an applied researcher who specializes in [the avant-garde](). My research abounds in several disciplines and often 
culminates on the intersection of two paradoxically analogus topics. I am serious about several forms of writing including 
technical, literary essays, reviews (literature, cinema, music), investigative journalism, genre fiction (micro, flash, novellas), and various forms of verse. 
I am pursuing a vast set of knowledge both wide, and deep with the hopes of having a unique enough skillset to spend my later 
years of contributing to some of the areas I view the most important in my limited world view. Mainly those involving the 
education of young children, and the refinement of education skills into adulthood as well as forming a slow academic lifestyle. 
I am also fairly interested in the development of world religions, (stress, and it's affects on memory and sleep), and a great deal 
of more philosophical questions such as the "why"? behind the continuation of humanity.
